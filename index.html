About Me

Hi I'm Shua!

I'm a singer, songwriter, music producer, and DJ.

As a young child, while most children dream of being an astronaut or superhero, all I wanted to be was a musician. Music was always, and still is, a huge part of my life.

I produced my first song when I was 16. This song was made in response to being sexually abused. The song talked about moving on, as well as giving strength to victims, and individuals going through hardships. I received a tremendous amount of feedback from this song. Many were from victims of sexual abuse saying how much the song helped them. You can view the song by clicking here.

After that, I continued working on my talents. I devoted a tremendous amount of time perfecting my producing skills. I also started voice lessons to perfect my singing. For the last three years, I have been producing songs for a handful of very satisfied clients now.

Just recently I worked on a demo to show my progress in producing and singing. You can listen to it here.

My dream is to be able to make music for a living. Music is my only passion and i will work as hard as it takes to make my dream a reality.

Thanks for checking out my site, I sincerely hope you enjoy my music!

I Am Shua

My name is Shua, and I am a singer, songwriter, music producer, and DJ.

While other kids aspired to be astronauts and superheroes, I dreamed of being a musician. Today, I am following that dream and doing what I love, creating music.

I produced my first song when I was 16. The song expressed moving on from difficult situations, and was meant to give strength to victims and individuals going through hardships and abuse. I received overwhelming feedback from this song, much of which were from victims of abuse who were inspired by the song. You can check out my first original piece here.

After my first success as a producer, I continued to develop my skills. I spent countless hours learning and practicing music production. I also took voice lessons to better my singing. Over the past three years, I have produced songs for a handful of very satisfied clients, including independent artists.

My dream is to make music for a living. I am devoted to pursuing my goal, and that enthusiasm carries over into all of my work.

I recently created a new song to showcase my progress in music production and singing. Click here to listen.

My dream is to be able to make music for a living. Music is my only passion and I will work as hard as it takes to make my dream a reality.

Thank you for checking out my site. I hope you enjoy my music!

DJ Shua

I began my career as a DJ by spinning records at house parties and in bars. After I established myself in these casual venues, I received requests to DJ at formal events.

I pride myself in my ability to read the crowd and to get everyone dancing. I offer complete sound setup as well as many lighting packages.

I am dedicated to serving my clients every step of the way. I am easily accessible and willing to address any questions or concerns to ensure client satisfaction. I use the highest quality equipment at all of my events, and I offer a personally curated library of music available on multiple formats. I also work with my clients to create custom remixes to help personalize their experience. My main priority is to make sure every client feels confident that their event will be a success.
